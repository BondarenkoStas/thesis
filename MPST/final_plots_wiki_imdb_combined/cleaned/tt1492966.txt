Louis C K
writes directs and stars in this show about a stand up comedian mostly performing late night shows at small clubs and restaurants
He is a single father living with his two school going daughters
Episodes focus on events in his everyday life and are bookended by clips from his stand up routine
C K
is the only regular in the show and even his daughters are portrayed by more than one actor
The style of humor is often sexual and racist with occasional use of vulgar language
C K
exercises total freedom in the episodes he creates and there are few boundaries established throughout the show with episodes dealing with such diverse topics as picking up the kids from school to doing drugs at his next door neighbor s
Most of his stand up routines mirror his real life and often have little or no association with the topic of the episode
