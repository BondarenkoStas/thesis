In the first season Kimmy Schmidt was in 8th grade when she was kidnapped for fifteen years by Reverend Richard Wayne Gary Wayne
The Reverend held Kimmy and three other women hostage in an underground bunker and convinced them that doomsday had come gone and had left them the sole survivors of humanity
Once the women are rescued they go on to appear on the Today Show in New York City
After the show Kimmy finds herself not wanting to return to Indiana but instead to start a new life in New York City
Roaming around the city Kimmy comes across landlady Lillian Kaushtupper
Kaushtupper offers Kimmy a chance to room with Titus Andromedon in her downstairs apartment
However Kimmy has to seek a job in order to get the apartment
Returning a child who has tried to steal candy Kimmy encounters his mother Jacqueline Voorhees a Manhattan trophy wife who mistakes her for a nanny and who Kimmy mistakes as someone trapped in a cult
Soon after Jacqueline hires Kimmy to be a nanny for her 10 year old son
As Season 1 continues Kimmy falls in love with a Vietnamese man named Dong from her G E D
class goes to court to testify against the Reverend and discovers how the world has changed in the 15 years she was locked up
In the second season Kimmy is sick of working for Jacqueline and acquires a job at a year round Christmas store and then as an Uber driver
She tries to get over Dong who enters a green card marriage with another G E D
student and eventually is deported
As Kimmy tries to move on so do Titus and Jacqueline who both find boyfriends
Titus begins dating a construction worker and Jaqueline begins dating a lawyer
Jacqueline also goes back to her Native American heritage and decides to take down the Redskins who her boyfriend s father happens to own
When Season 2 ends Titus leaves to be a performer on a cruise and Lillian protests the invasion of hipsters on her neighborhood while Kimmy makes amends with her mother after advice from her therapist before receiving a phone call from The Reverend in prison telling her that they need to get a divorce
