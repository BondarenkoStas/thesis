A power struggle at Kyne inc ensues after the death of media magnate Amos Kyne who turned his corporation over to his sole heir foppish son Walter
Rather than run the Kyne inc himself Walter decides to challenge the heads of its 3 divisions
Their assignment score an exclusive story on a serial killer terrorizing women in New York City the Kyne organization s newspaper has dubbed The Lipstick Killer
Whoever identifies him before the police will be rewarded the title of Executive Director

One of the 3 newspaper editor Jon Day Griffith has an ally in high profile Kyne reporter and television personality Edward Mobley
Wire service chief Mark Loving recruits star writer Mildred Donner as his eyes and ears while third contender Harry Kritzer carries on a secret affair with Walter Kyne s wife Dorothy
Mobley becomes engaged to Loving s secretary Nancy Liggett
Receiving inside information from his police friend Lt Kaufman Mobley taunts the killer on TV using Nancy as bait
Nancy and Dorothy who live across the hall from each other are all placed in serious jeopardy while one of the 3 contenders for the head position of Kyne inc wins another has a surprise in store
