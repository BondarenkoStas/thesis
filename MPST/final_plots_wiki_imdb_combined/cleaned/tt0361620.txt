Kasemiire grows up in poverty
But her father is able to send her and her siblings to school
This he does amidst scorn from other men who thinks all a woman has to do is to work in the kitchen
Kasemiire gets pregnant in school and is abandoned by the father of her child
Kasemire has to work to support herself and her child
She impresses a politician who offers to help her by taking her to work in the city
Things do not go to plan after the politicians s husband tries to rape her
Kasemiire seeks refuge from the church where with the help of a sympathetic nun she goes back to school under their care
She goes up to university
It is at the university that she meets the father of her child and then the hatred she had concealed comes to the surface
