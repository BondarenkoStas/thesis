Dr Henry Jekyll where the doctor is being feted prior to his engagement to the austere Miss Fanny Osborne
The guests arrive and are various dignitaries and officials
After a meal the doctor is summoned to his laboratory to get his will
He returns to the living room when a scream is heard where one of his guests has been discovered raped and murdered
Henry Jekyll transforms to his alter ego Mr Hyde by taking a bath filled with a chemical cocktail
He emerges physically transformed
His alter ego has none of the restrictions of morality and he proceeds to rape and torture various guests
Eventually Fanny witnesses one such transformation
She leaps into the bath to be transformed as well
The two transformed leave the house and in an carriage they depart whilst undertaking blood letting of each other and love making
