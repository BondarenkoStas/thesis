When one of the personnel stationed at the base goes on a rampage two military operatives Capt
Sam Cage and Maj Callie O Grady drop in to search the base
They discover two survivors medical officer Capt
Jennifer Wells and technical officer Lieut
Brian Shebanski

Cage sees what appears to be another survivor and starts a chase through the corridors taking an elevator to the second floor
