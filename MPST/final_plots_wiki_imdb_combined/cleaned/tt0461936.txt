Don is a ruthless criminal wanted by police in many countries
DCP D Silva and Detective Malik are two cops who will do anything to capture Don
Don soon brutally murders one of his own men Ramesh as well as his fiance Kamini
By doing so Don makes an enemy out of Ramesh s sister Roma who swears to kill Don no matter what it takes Don is soon injured in a police pursuit and is admitted to hospital in a coma
D Silva tracks down a look alike of Don named Vijay
He persuades Vijay to enter Don s gang posing as Don
Vijay agrees to hand over a disc to D Silva a disc which contains vital information about the gang this disc may be a way for the cops to arrest the gang
Roma attempts to kill Vijay but stops when she learns about D Silva s plan During a police raid Vijay and the gang members are all arrested by Malik
During the raid D Silva is killed
This causes problems for Vijay D Silva was the only person who could prove Vijay is not the real Don
Will Vijay be able to prove his innocence
