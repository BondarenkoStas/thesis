Ramachandra Rao Ramu falls in love with a beautiful girl Lalitha who is also his cousin
He loves her so deeply that he wants to marry her at any cost
His father is the Head of the village his mother a housewife
Due to some internal disputes between Ramachandra Rao s parents and Lalitha s parents Lalitha s father denies the proposal
Later according to his parents wish Ramachandra Rao is forced to marry another girl Gouri the daughter of a lawyer
But he refuses to accept her as his wife as he is already in love with Lalitha and does not behave properly with Gouri
His parents observe their son s behavior and leave for pilgrimage thinking that their absence will bring both wife and husband together
Gouri is very obedient though she is well educated and rich
She loves her husband very much and expects the same from him like any other wife
But her husband continues to neglect her
She even asks him the reason for rejecting her
He says that he is helpless and asks her to go to her mother s place
She refuses to leave and continues to stay in the same house along with her husband
She prays to her favorite God daily with great devotion
God listens to her at the end and her husband accepts her as his wife and gives her all his love
Watch the movie to discover why the hero suddenly changes his mind and accepts his wife
The comedy between the lawyer K V Chalam and Allu Ramalingaiah is enjoyable
As a whole the film is a wholesome family entertainment movie
