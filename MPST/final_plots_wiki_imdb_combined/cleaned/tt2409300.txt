Nomura Shuhei a young charismatic and well liked Japanese executive turns out to have a dark side
He kills people and documenting them in a video
Besides that Nomura also uploaded videos of the killing on the internet in order to be seen by all people for the sake of personal satisfaction
On the other side of the earth Bayu Aditya an ambitious Indonesian journalist who was broke
His obsession to uncover the case of a politician named Dharma makes his relationship with his wife Dina and his career fell apart
When his life was filled with problems Bayu saw one of Nomura s video and began to find his other side
Bayu was inspired to do the same thing to be a serial killer
He started to form his own character and start killing in the name of justice then uploading the videos to the internet
Unexpectedly Bayu s video got a response as well as Nomura s video
Then they were connected via the Internet and the bond between them became more complicated
Since then Nomura decided to see Bayu
