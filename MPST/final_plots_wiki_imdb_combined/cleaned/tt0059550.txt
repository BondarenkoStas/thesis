Segment Déjà vu It s time for summer examinations at the University and everyone is cramming for the exams
Shurik is looking desperately for lecture notes and finally sees them in the hands of a girl on a streetcar Lida who is a student of the same University
As Shurik follows her reading the notebook over her shoulder they become so deeply absorbed in reading the notes that Lida never looks up instinctively assuming that Shurik is one of her female coeds
